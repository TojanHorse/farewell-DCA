Future Endeavors

As the Batch of 2023-24 steps into the future, their journey continues with remarkable opportunities and ambitions. Our seniors are set to make significant contributions in various fields, reflecting the knowledge and skills they have acquired during their time with us. Here are some of the exciting paths our graduates are pursuing:
Startups and Entrepreneurship:
Several of our entrepreneurial-minded seniors are venturing into the startup ecosystem, launching their own companies and innovative projects. With their creative ideas and entrepreneurial spirit, they are ready to make a mark in the business world.


Education:
Many of our seniors are advancing their academic journey by pursuing master's and doctoral programs at prestigious universities around the world. Their thirst for knowledge and innovation continues to drive them towards new academic heights.

Tech Industry:
A significant number of our graduates are joining leading tech companies, ready to apply their expertise in software development, data science, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence. They are poised to become the next generation of innovators and leaders in the technology sector.

Research and Development:
Our graduates are also contributing to cutting-edge research in various domains of computer science. Their work in R&D is set to drive technological advancements and create solutions to some of the most pressing challenges of our time.
Community and Social Impact:
Some of our seniors are dedicating their skills to non-profit organizations and community initiatives, using technology to make a positive impact on society. Their commitment to social change and community development is truly inspiring.
